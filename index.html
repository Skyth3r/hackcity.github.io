FAQ

Are tickets free?

Yes, 100%!

But what if I can't code?

We want you to come along anyway! Regardless of what you think of your technical skills, it's a great opportunity to learn new skills!

What if I study outside of the UK?

You are still welcome to join us! However, we cannot sponsor visas.

When and where will it be?

It's on the 25th and 26th of March, and will be held at the Pavilion (Northampton Square entrance).

What do I bring?

Bring your laptop, phone, chargers and don't forget your student ID.
